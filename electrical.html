Electrical Engineering

What is Electrical Engineering?

Electrical Engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It involves designing, developing, and maintaining electrical systems and devices.

Key Subjects in Electrical Engineering

  • Circuit Theory
  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Signal Processing
  • Microelectronics
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Electrical Machines

Career Opportunities

Electrical engineers can work in industries such as power generation, telecommunications, electronics, and automation. They can pursue roles like power systems engineer, electronics designer, or control systems engineer.

Eligibility

To pursue Electrical Engineering, students must complete 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. They must also clear entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, or state-level engineering exams.
